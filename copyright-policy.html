Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy describes how Alien Adventure: Galactic Odyssey (“the Game”) handles information. We designed the Game to be enjoyed without collecting personal data.

1. What We Collect

  • No personal information by default. The Game does not require accounts, names, emails, or phone numbers.
  • Basic device/usage data (website only). Your web host (e.g., GitHub Pages) may log standard technical data such as IP address, browser type, and pages viewed for security and performance.

2. How We Use Information

  • Operate and improve the website and the Game.
  • Diagnose issues and protect against abuse.

3. Ads, Analytics, and Third Parties

We do not use ad-wall services (e.g., “lootdesk/lootlabs”) or third-party advertising SDKs in the Game or on this site. We also do not embed third-party analytics scripts. Your hosting provider may collect minimal logs as noted above.

4. Children’s Privacy

The Game is not directed to children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child provided personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

5. Data Retention

We do not store personal data. Basic server logs (handled by the hosting provider) are retained per their policies.

6. Your Rights

If you contact us with personal information (e.g., email), you can request deletion at any time.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes to the Game or legal requirements. Significant changes will be posted here.
