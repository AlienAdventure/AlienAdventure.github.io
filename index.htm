Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy describes how Alien Adventure: Galactic Odyssey (“the Game”) handles information. We designed the Game to be enjoyed without collecting personal data.

1. What We Collect

  • No personal information is required to play.
  • Basic device/usage data (like IP, browser type) may be logged by GitHub Pages for hosting/security.

2. How We Use Information

  • Operate and improve the website and the Game.
  • Protect against abuse and troubleshoot issues.

3. Ads, Analytics, and Third Parties

We do not use lootdesk/lootlabs or any ad-wall services. No analytics scripts are embedded. Only minimal server logs may be collected by the host.

4. Children’s Privacy

The Game is not directed to children under 13.

5. Data Retention

We do not store personal data. Hosting logs follow GitHub policies.
