Alien Adventure

Embark on an intergalactic journey as a green player, leaping past enemies to complete thrilling levels!

About the Game

Alien Adventure is an action-packed game where you guide a green player past enemies and navigate thrilling levels. Easy to play but hard to master!

Gameplay Controls

Laptop/PC
Android

- Up Arrow: Jump
- Left Arrow: Move Left
- Right Arrow: Move Right
- Down Arrow: Descend ladders

- Up Button: Jump
- Left Button: Move Left
- Right Button: Move Right
- Key Button: Unlock doors when near
