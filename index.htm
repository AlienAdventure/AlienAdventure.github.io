Alien Adventure

Embark on an intergalactic journey as a green player, leaping past enemies to complete thrilling levels!

About the Game

Alien Adventure is a fast-paced, action-packed game where you control a green player who leaps over enemies to reach the end of each level or a door. With simple yet addictive gameplay mechanics, Alien Adventure provides endless fun for players of all skill levels!

Gameplay Controls

Laptop/PC
Android

- **Up Arrow**: Jump
- **Left Arrow**: Move Left
- **Right Arrow**: Move Right
- **Down Arrow**: Use ladders

- **Up Button**: Jump
- **Left Button**: Move Left
- **Right Button**: Move Right
- **Key Button**: Unlock doors when standing in front of them
